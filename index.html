About Me

I am an IT undergraduate (2015-2019) from G.G.S. Indraprastha University, New Delhi. I am a self learned full stack web developer with two years of experience. I have created websites ranging from music sharing platform, health information lookup to escrow services and bitcoin wallet. I am a Python language lover. I have participated in some of the biggest hackathons across India - Angelhack (Delhi), Rajasthan Hackathon 2.0 (Kota), Bharathacks (Delhi), etc.
I have been working in the field of blockchain and cryptocurrency since second half of 2017 and have created a bitcoin wallet form scratch and many smart contracts. I am a blockchain and cryptocurency advocate and developer.

Getting into the field of blockchain has never prevented my love for exploration in web developement. Infact I am more curious than ever to know how internet works and have been fascinated by all the developments and innovations that have taken place since its origin.

I am a very strong supporter of #NetNeutrality and Freedom of free speech and ideas. Also, I am very much inspired by the lives and works. of Elon Musk, Aaron Swartz, Julian Assange and Edward Snowden.
